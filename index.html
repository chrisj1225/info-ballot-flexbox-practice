Trump's Views/Ideologies


Covid-19

  • Created a task force (incl. Dr. Anthony Fauci, the director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases)
  • Implemented stringent travel restrictions to contain the outbreak
  • Initially stated he didn't believe making masks mandatory across the country was necessary.
  • Wanted to 'slow down' covid-19 testing as it would lead to a higher number of reported positive cases.

Health Care

  • First executive order aims to 'protect Americans with pre-existing conditions' while tearing down the Affordable Care Act
  • Second executive address surprise medical billing
  • Give Americans access to alternative policies with fewer protections & benefits.
  • Seeking to invalidate the landmark health care law.
  • Plans to reduce drug prices
  • Require hospitals to post the rates they pay to health insurers

Economy

  • Signed largest emergency aid package in US history in response to the economic impact of the coronavirus pandemic
  • Signed into law a coronavirus relief package
  • Increased Medicaid funding, certain tax credits and expanded food assistance
  • 2017 tax cut drastically reduced rates for indivudals and businesses
  • Federal budget deficit rose to nearly $1 trillion in fiscal year 2019
  • Campaign to eliminate national debt altogether by the end of a second term

Police Reform

  • "President of law and order"
  • Condemned efforts to defund police departments
  • Created a tracking program to encourage localities to submit information on officers found to have used excessive force
  • Federal grants towards police depts. that are credentialed for having use of force & de-escalation policies
  • Banned the use of chokeholds, except when lethal force is authorized
  • Program that pairs social workers with police to answer mental health and homelessness calls

Gun Violence

  • Expanding background checks.
  • Repeatedly points to mental health and hate as the underlying issues behind gun violence
  • After the 2017 Las Vegas shooting, ordered Dept. of Justice to ban bump stocks (attachments that make semi-automatic rifles fire continuously)
  • Backed "red flag" gun laws on the state level

Immigration

  • During 2016 campaign, proposed the construction of a wall along US-Mexico border
  • Suspended the entry of people from a number of Muslim-majority countries for 90 days
  • 2018 "zero tolerance" policy allowed criminal prosecutions of adults who illegally crossed the border
  • Separated thousands of families at the border as parents were detained
  • Proposed a merit-based immigration system for green card holders
  • Ended Obama-era protections for undocumented immigrants who were brought into the country as children

Education

  • Vowed to fix student loan debt
  • Automatic loan forgiveness for veterans with disabilities
  • Urged congress to cap student loan borrowing
  • School choice tax credit ("Education Freedom Scholarships"> that students can use to attend public or private schools outside of their districts
  • Rescinded several Obama-era policies including those that promoted racial diversity in schools & protections for transgender students in public schools
  • Rolled back two rules intended to hold for-profit colleges accountable

Foreign Relations

  • Has made a series of unpredictable moves on trade
  • Imposed tariffs against allies such as the European Union
  • Engaged in a 2-year trade war with China, imposing retaliatory tariffs that have hit American farmers, importers and manufacturers
  • Announced plans to withdraw from the Trans-Pacific Partnership Negotiations and Agreement
  • Renegotiated the North American Free Trade Agreement, the trade pact with Canada and Mexico

Climate Crisis

  • Withdrew the United States from the Paris climate accord - a landmark 2015 deal on global warming targets
  • This sent a message to the rest of the world that the US would not be leading the global fight against climate change
  • Does not view climate change as a top priority
  • Pushed to open Alaska's Arctic National Wildlife Refuge to oil and gas exploration, as well as waters along the East and Pacific coasts
  • EPA announced it would no longer require oil and gas companies to install monitors to detect methane leaks from new wells, tanks and pipelines

Biden's Views/Ideologies


Covid-19

  • Free testing for all americans
  • Hire 100,000 people for national contact tracing
  • Increase number of drive-thru testing sites
  • Provide financial support for retaining & rehiring employees
  • Guaranteed paid leave for anybody with covid-19 or who is caring for someone with the virus
  • Mandate everyone wear a mask in public

Health Care

  • Greatly expand Obamacare's subsidies: Make private insurance policies more affordable
  • Create new "public option" (similar to Medicare) that people can buy into
  • Make public option available for those with private/employer insurance.
  • People with private/employer insurance can "join up for a Medicaid-Medicare-like provision in the law"

Economy

  • Build economy that "rewards work, not just wealth"
  • Repeal tax cuts enacted by Trump Administration
  • $15 minimum wage
  • Expand access to affordable education
  • Raise top individual income tax rate to 39.5%
  • Raise corporate tax rate from 21% to 28%
  • Tax credits for people with children, seniors and disabled people.
  • Spur manufacturing in clean energy, infrastructure & health care

Police Reform

  • Does not support calls to "defund the police"
  • Supports the urgent need for reform - including funding for public schools, summer programs and mental health and substance abuse treatment separate from police funding
  • More funding for police reforms such as body cameras and training on community policing approaches
  • $300 million in funding for Community Oriented Policing Services program -- pays for training on community policing approaches.

Gun Violence

  • Push to ban so-called assault weapons
  • Assault weapons are a threat to US national security
  • Federal buyback program to take guns off the streets
  • Supports universal background checks
  • Focus on gun manufacturers

Immigration

  • Supports a pathway to citizenship for undocumented immigrants
  • Called on congress to immediately grant citizenship to some undocumented immigrants brought to the US as children
  • Undocumented immigrants with no criminal records "should not be the focus of deportation"
  • Opposes decriminalizing crossing the border without documentation
  • Give people who are seeking asylum a chance to make their case

Education

  • Proposed education plan to increase funding for schools in low-income areas, help teachers pay off student loans and double the number of health professionals in schools
  • Closing $23 billion funding gap between majority white and nonwhite school districts
  • Cut student loan debt by waiving $10,000 per year for up to 5 years for those in public service work
  • Those earning less than $25,000 owe nothing on undergraduate federal student loans
  • Halve student loan payments from 10% to 5%

Foreign Relations

  • Extensive foreign policy experience from 8 years serving in the White House
  • Former chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee
  • Vowed to restore pre-Trump international norms
  • Call among world's democracies and tech and social media companies to seek common agenda to protect shared values
  • Combat surveillance, censorship and spread of hate speech
  • While in the Senate, voted to authorize war in Iraq in 2002 and has since apologized for it

Climate Crisis

  • Proposed spending $2 trillion over four years on clean energy projects and ending carbon emissions from power plants by 2035
  • Plans to urgently reduce fossil fuel consumptions to combat climate change
  • Task force to combat climate change made up of members appointed by Biden and VT Sen. Bernie Sanders and co-chaired by NY Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, a chief proponent of the Green New Deal
